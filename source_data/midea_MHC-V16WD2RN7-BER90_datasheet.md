# Midea M-Thermal Nature Mono R290 16 kW — MHC-V16WD2RN7-BER90

Air-to-water monoblock heat pump, R290, 3-phase (380-415 V / 3 Ph / 50 Hz).

## Rated heating points (EN 14511)

| Condition | Heat output (kW) | El. input (kW) | COP |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|------|
| A-7 / W35 | 13.10            | 4.852          | 2.70 |
| A7 / W35  | 15.50            | 3.444          | 4.50 |
| A7 / W45  | 15.50            | 4.247          | 3.65 |
| A7 / W55  | 16.00            | 5.246          | 3.05 |

## Seasonal performance (EN 14825, average climate)

- SCOP @ W35: 4.72 (A+++)
- SCOP @ W55: 3.86 (A+++)

## Operating envelope

- Heating ambient range: -25 … +35 °C
- Flow (supply) water temperature heating: 25 … 80 °C  (so 55 °C and 58 °C are well within spec)
- DHW water temperature: 20 … 70 °C
- Backup electric heater: 9 kW (3 + 3 + 3 steps)
- Refrigerant: R290 (GWP 3), charge 1.5 kg
- Weight: 169 kg
